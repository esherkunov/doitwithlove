We offer you to make a personal experiment and pump your actions with love.
The application is based on statistical probability of giving advice to commit an act with love in proper time. That will pull you out of standard behavior, and probably, it will become your life language.

Your life language is always individual, somebody will get the notification during quarrel, somebody will get it while passing by a hungry tramp, somebody – during the meeting that special someone, who you are confused to talk to.

Of course, these notifications won’t be timely, but these situations will happen during a month. Moreover, looking forward to getting these notification can inspire you.

For setting up you just need to choose time slot on twenty-four-hour dial plate, when the notification can be shown, write down your name and push the button “on”.
